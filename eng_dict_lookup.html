• 输入框：~开头则是模糊查, /开头是按音标查。
  • 音标输入,用下面代号时要空格隔开：
    • ae=æ, a:=ɑ
    • sh=ʃ, 3=ʒ
    • sth=θ, zth=ð
    • ng=ŋ
  • 音标说明：
    • /ʊ/ as in put, /u/=/u:/ as in zoo
    • /ɪ/ as in little, /i/=/i:/ as in meat
    • /æ/ as in bad, /ɛ/ as in bed
    • /ə/ as in about, /ɝ/=/ɝ:/=/ə:/ as in bird
    • /ɑ/=/ɑ:/ as in march, /ɔ/ as in boy or log
    • /aʊ/ as in about, /aɪ/ as in bike, /eɪ/ as in gate, /oʊ/ as in boat
